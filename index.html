Redefining Lifestyle

Experience Luxury Living

Located in the heart of Streeterville, a neighborhood with a history as rich as its shopping areas, Lifestyle Luxury Apartments offers a one-of-a-kind experience. Overlooking the Florence River, Lifestyle Luxury Apartments offers some of the largest apartments in the city. Equipped with custom kitchens, in-unit laundry rooms, exposed brick and over 100 unique layouts, the building has something for everyone.

Residents will also enjoy laying out on the 10,000 square foot rooftop SkyDeck, working out in our state-of-the-art athletic room, playing pool in the gaming room, watching a movie in the private screening room and having cocktails with friends while looking out onto the Riverwalk from the waterfront lounge. Lifestyle Luxury Apartments – a one-of-a-kind building for a one-of-a-kind city.
